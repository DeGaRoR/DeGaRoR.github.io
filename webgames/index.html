Meet the team

After about a decade of traditional work, Denis and Julien decided that life should have more to offer than toxic office culture, long commutes and being far away from what we really liked in life. We have founded NewCo to create free games, but also to put in light the digital talents of our friends and collaborators. It's only the start of the road, but we are very excited to contribute, at our mini scale, to a better world where work and private life do not have to be incompatible anymore. Thomas joined us soon after as lead artist for the music, sounds and asset design.
Wish us well, and get in touch, we'd love to hear from you
