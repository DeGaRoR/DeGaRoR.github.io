Meet the team

After about a decade of traditional work, Denis and Julien decided that life should have more to offer. We have founded NewCo to create free games, but also to put in light the digital talents of our friends and collaborators. It's only the start of the road, but we are very excited to contribute, at our mini scale, to a better world where work and private life concile better. The God Engineer

Genre: RTS Casual
Platform: Mobile Computer
Status: 30%
Pitch

You are a well-educated person, holding vast knowledge of modern technology. No luck though, after being striken by lightning (or whatever floats your boat), you wake up in -10,000 BC. That's gonna be tough holidays! After the few first days miserably trying to find your way around, you decide tthat it is actually a great opportunity. With your current knowledge, you can bring great enlightment to the world. Or you can conquer and dominate. Or you can just pretend you're god, maybe that's gonna fly. Beware though, the inhabitants might not be as harmless as you think, and not everyone welcomes enlightment.

Game mechanics

The game is essentially a mini-4x, with a survival aspect. You handle difficult moral choices which have significant impact on the evolution of the game. The unique mechanism is that you will have to choose whether you want to exploit your existing knowledge for perfroming tasks on your own, or spend your time training your prehistoric citizens so your model scales up eventually.
